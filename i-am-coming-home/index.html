I am coming home...

This song has really touched me. It will not be that long from now until I leave my beloved exchange country as well as my nationality – India.
Unfortunately, this is song is not talking about my place of origin abroad there, in Slovakia. It is talking about the place which, during one year, has become my real home – India herself.

I must be honest I truly cried while listening to this song. It reminds me of leaving my beloved home, my beloved families and friends, and after all this beautiful place, Tamil Nadu, for a very long time. Hoping to come back soon!
